About me



I, Arman Hayk Mesropyan, was born on July 17, 1999 in the city of Eյmiadzin, Armavir region. In 2005, I entered and graduated from Rafael Patkanyan Secondary School №9 in the city in 2013. In the same year, I entered and graduated from Maxim Gorki High School №5 in the city in 2016. In 2016, I was admitted and in 2020 I graduated from the Department of Industrial and Civil Construction of the Faculty of Construction of the National University of Architecture and Construction of Armenia (NUACA) (diploma of excellence) with a bachelor's qualification. In the same year, I entered and in 2022 I graduated from the above-mentioned department's master's program (diploma of excellence), receiving a master's qualification. Along with my studies, which started in 2020, I have been engaged in scientific research work, I am the co-author of the scientific article "DIGITAL ANALYSIS OF BUILDINGS SCHEMES WITH DIFFERENT ARRANGEMENT OF DIAPHRAGMS AND THEIR OPTIMAL APPLICATION", and I am also the author of "ANALYTICAL AND DIGITAL ANALYSIS OF METAL BUNKERS AND DETERMINATION OF THE THICKNESS OF THE BUNKER WALL" and co-author and author respectively of the scientific articles "DETERMINATION OF THE OPTIMALITY OF THE USE OF STEEL-REINFORCED CONCRETE STRUCTURES IN THE FRAME BEARING SYSTEM", which are published among the scientific works of the National Academy of Sciences, since June 2021, I have been working at the Mountain Metallurgy Institute(LMI) CJSC as an engineer-designer. , where I still work. I am not married, I have no children.
